# 🏨 AtliQ Grands Hospitality Analysis 

This project presents an end-to-end data analysis of **AtliQ Grands**, a fictional hotel chain operating across India. The analysis was performed using **Python** in **Jupyter Notebook**, leveraging 
**Pandas** for data manipulation and **Matplotlib** and **Seaborn** for data visualization to uncover key business insights and trends.

---

## 📊 Project Overview

The project involves analyzing hotel booking data to generate actionable insights into business performance, occupancy trends, and customer behavior. Key activities include:

* Data import, cleaning, and preprocessing
* Exploratory data analysis and transformation
* Generation of analytical reports and key metrics
* Development of visualizations to support data-driven decision-making

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze revenue trends across cities and hotel categories.
* Compare occupancy rates by city, room category, and day type.
* Identify top-performing properties and booking platforms.
* Study customer ratings and cancellation patterns.
* Provide actionable recommendations to improve revenue and occupancy.

## 📈 Key Insights

* Mumbai generated the highest revenue.
* Delhi achieved the highest occupancy and customer ratings.
* Luxury hotels contributed more revenue than Business hotels.
* Weekend occupancy (72.3%) was significantly higher than weekday occupancy (50.9%).
* AtliQ Exotica was the highest revenue-generating property.
* Mumbai recorded the highest number of booking cancellations.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Improve weekday occupancy through targeted promotions.
* Reduce cancellations, particularly in Mumbai.
* Expand successful luxury hotel strategies.
* Improve the performance of underperforming properties.
* Increase direct online bookings to reduce dependency on third-party platforms.

---

## 📌 Results

AtliQ Grands maintained stable business performance, with Luxury hotels acting as the primary revenue drivers. Mumbai emerged as the strongest revenue market, while Delhi achieved the highest occupancy and customer satisfaction. Strategic improvements in pricing, occupancy management, and cancellation reduction can further enhance profitability.
